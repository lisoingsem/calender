# Brand Guide

## Identity

- **Name**: `calendar`
- **Namespace**: `Lisoing\Calendar`
- **Tagline**: *Universal Lunar & Solar Calendar with Worldwide Holidays — Powered by Community*
- **Tone**: Developer-friendly, inclusive, precise, and globally minded.

## Logo & Icon Direction

- Minimal calendar silhouette combining a clock face to imply time awareness.
- Incorporate lunar (crescent moon) and solar (sun rays) elements to visualise dual calendar support.
- Optional globe outline communicates worldwide coverage.
- Use monochrome or duotone colour palette for clarity at small sizes.

## Colours

| Name            | Hex       | Usage                                  |
|-----------------|-----------|----------------------------------------|
| Eclipse Navy    | `#1F2A44` | Primary text, icons, headings          |
| Solar Gold      | `#F5A623` | Highlights, buttons, icon accent       |
| Lunar Silver    | `#D8DCE3` | Background panels, dividers            |
| Aurora Teal     | `#1BB0A6` | Links, success states                  |

## Typography

- Headings: Inter, SemiBold
- Body: Inter, Regular
- Code: JetBrains Mono

## Messaging Pillars

1. **Universal Coverage** – Lunar and solar calendars, worldwide holidays.
2. **Community-Led** – Contributors add new calendars, countries, and translations.
3. **Developer-First** – Laravel-ready, PSR-12 compliant, tested and documented.
4. **Inclusive** – Multilingual, culturally diverse holiday data.

## Usage Guidelines

- README header: lockup with logo, tagline, and badges (`ci`, `coverage`, `contributors`).
- Social banners: highlight global contributors, emphasise “Powered by Community”.
- Presentation decks: open with roadmap and contributor impact metrics.
