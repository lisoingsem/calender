# Support Resources

We want everyone to be successful with `calendar`. Below are the support channels and expected response times.

## Documentation

- [`README.md`](README.md) covers installation, usage, and roadmap.
- [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md) explains contribution workflows.
- Additional guides live in the `docs/` directory.

## Community Channels

- **GitHub Issues**: Bug reports and feature requests. We triage within 5 business days.
- **GitHub Discussions**: Share ideas, ask “how do I?” questions, and collaborate with the community.
- **Discord** *(coming soon)*: Real-time chat for contributors and maintainers.

## Priority Support

Commercial or time-sensitive support can be requested via [support@lisoing.dev](mailto:support@lisoing.dev). Please include the package version, PHP/Laravel versions, and reproduction steps.

## Response Times

- Security reports: acknowledgement within 48 hours.
- Bug reports: first response within 5 business days.
- PR reviews: initial feedback within 7 business days.

We are an open-source project; response times are best-effort and may vary during holidays or major releases.
